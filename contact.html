Booking Process FAQs

What is included in our booking?

We take care of the essentials so you can focus on enjoying your event. Every Grill Seekers booking comes with:

  • Disposable plates, cutlery, napkins, and condiments
  • A fully equipped food station gazebo
  • Chafing trays to keep your food warm and fresh throughout service
  • Professional setup and breakdown
  • Friendly, experienced staff

How do we Book?

Our booking process is simple and straightforward:

  1. You fill out the booking form selecting the package you want to enquire about.
  2. We'll create a personalised quote and send it over for your review.
  3. Once accepted, we'll provide deposit payment instructions.
  4. We'll confirm all details and send you a booking confirmation.

What is the minimum booking?

Our minimum booking is 30 heads across all packages. This ensures we can provide the best service and experience for your event.
