1. Definitions

Client: the person or organisation making the booking. Event: the catered event. Packages: Classic British Barbecue (BB), Signature Feast, and Gourmet Experience (G).

2. Booking Process

  • Complete the booking form and select your preferred package.
  • We issue a personalised quote for your review.
  • On acceptance, we provide deposit payment instructions.
  • Once the deposit is received, we issue an invoice and your event date is secured.
