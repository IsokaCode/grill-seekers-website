The Grill Seekers Story

Where Smoke Meets Soul
Grill Seekers isn't just a BBQ and catering company; we're a full-blown flavour movement. Born from backyard banter and a burning passion for bold eats, we turned a love for grilling into a culinary experience that's anything but ordinary. What started as a few fiery cookouts among friends quickly evolved into a brand that brings theatre to the table and joy to every bite.

We don't just serve food, we serve moments. Our events are interactive, high-energy, and unapologetically fun. Expect sizzling sounds, smoky aromas, and a team that's as charismatic as the cuisine. Whether it's a corporate lunch, wedding feast, or street food pop-up, Grill Seekers transforms any space into a flavour arena.

Why We're Different

At Grill Seekers, we believe exceptional BBQ starts with exceptional ingredients. Every cut of meat is sourced from London's prestigious Smithfield Market, while our fresh fish comes directly from Billingsgate. We use only restaurant-grade charcoal and premium woods like cherry and pimento to create the perfect smoke.

Inclusive Excellence

We proudly serve all communities with dedicated halal and kosher preparations, ensuring zero cross-contamination. Our team understands that great food brings people together, regardless of dietary requirements.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

FOOD FAQS

How does choosing my package work?

You choose your BBQ package, then pick 3 meats and 2 salads to build your main course. Salads and sides are served buffet style. Platters are available upon request for a small extra cost.

I've had a look through the menus, just wondering, what do "BB" and "G" stand for besides each choice?

(BB) stands for British Barbecue option, which includes 3 meat options and costs £20 per head.
(G) stands for Gourmet Barbecue option, which also includes 3 meat options and costs £30 per head.

How much are your sides please?

Sides are available from £2.50 per head.

What is the minimum heads per booking?

The minimum booking is for 40 seats.

Can I book canapés as a standalone?

The canapés menu cannot be booked on its own; it's only offered as an add-on when you also book the Grill Seekers BBQ main menu. If you only want light bites or evening snacks, you would need to commit to the full daytime BBQ package to access the canapés.

Exception for Corporate Events: Large corporate bookings are an exception and are eligible to book the canapés menu independently, without the daytime BBQ.

Is the left-over food ours to keep?

Yes, if there's any food left at the end of your event, they are happy to hand it over. You just need to bring along some containers or platters, and they will fill them up for you.

Do you cater for different dietary requirements?

Separate grills for halal, kosher and vegan menus mean everyone eats worry-free. After every event, we give each BBQ a full wash and steam-clean, so there's zero cross-contamination and total peace of mind.

How long do we serve food for?

We serve for a full hour and a half, which gives everyone plenty of time to eat, and come back for seconds (or thirds!). If there's any meat left over, we'll pop it on platters along with any remaining rolls and salads, so you and your guests can keep grazing after service wraps up.

Can we have a BBQ in any weather condition?

Rain, shine, or snow, we bring the heat no matter the forecast. Our setup includes gazebos to keep your food covered and protected, so your event runs smoothly in any condition.

COMPANY FAQS

Are you qualified & insured?

Professional Food Safety Standards: Every member of the Grill Seekers team holds accredited food hygiene certifications, ensuring they follow rigorous protocols for handling, preparing, and serving food. This commitment to proper training reduces the risk of contamination or foodborne illness and demonstrates that the company prioritizes guest health at every event.

Comprehensive Liability Coverage: Carrying £10million in public liability insurance and £10 million in Employee Liability means that if any accidental injury or property damage occurs during service, the policy will cover associated costs. This level of insurance is a hallmark of Grill Seekers reputable caterers and gives clients peace of mind that unforeseen incidents won't become their financial responsibility.

How far do you travel?

Based in Watford, Hertfordshire we serve most of the Southeast of England, traveling up to 3 hours from Watford for standard bookings. For larger corporate weddings and events, we're happy to go further — just note that a mileage fee applies for bookings over 25 miles from Watford.

BOOKING PROCESS FAQS

What is included in our booking?

We take care of the essentials so you can focus on enjoying your event. Every Grill Seekers booking comes with:

  • Disposable plates, cutlery, napkins, and condiments
  • A fully equipped food station gazebo
  • Chafing trays to keep your food warm and fresh throughout service

How do we Book?

To get started, just email us your event details. We'll create a personalised quote and send it over for your review. If you accept the quote, we'll send you a booking form to complete and return. Once we receive your form, we'll provide deposit payment instructions. After your deposit is paid, we'll issue an invoice and secure your event date. It's that simple your BBQ experience will be officially booked and ready to go.

GENERAL FAQS

Arrival times and leaving times

We usually arrive approximately three hours before your scheduled serving time to ensure everything is set up and ready to go. While we aim to be in place before your guests arrive, there may be rare occasions where this isn't possible, but we'll always keep things efficient and low-key. Once finished, we'll quietly pack down and leave without disrupting the flow of your event.
