Our Story - From Backyard Dreams to Professional BBQ Catering

Where Smoke Meets Soul
Grill Seekers isn't just a BBQ and catering company; we're a full-blown flavour movement. Born from backyard banter and a burning passion for bold eats, we turned a love for grilling into a culinary experience that's anything but ordinary. What started as weekend cookouts with friends has evolved into Watford's premier BBQ catering service, trusted by couples, corporations, and communities across Hertfordshire.

Our journey began with a simple belief: great BBQ isn't just about the food—it's about the experience, the atmosphere, and the memories we create together. Every event we cater becomes a celebration of flavour, bringing people together around the warmth of perfectly grilled meats and the joy of shared meals.
