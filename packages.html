Booking Information & Requirements

Everything you need to know about booking our BBQ catering services

Booking Information

For bookings or more information, please contact our team. We gladly accommodate special requests and dietary needs. Our barbecue packages require a minimum booking of 30 guests to ensure the best service and experience.

Gourmet Package Table Service

Table service is complimentary for bookings of over 60 guests. For bookings below 60 guests, a service fee will apply.

Advance Booking

We recommend booking at least 4-6 weeks in advance for weddings and large events

Minimum Guests

Minimum 30 guests for all packages to ensure quality service and equipment setup

Venue Access

We require vehicle access to your venue for equipment delivery and setup

Power Requirements

Access to electrical outlets for our equipment (generators available if needed)
