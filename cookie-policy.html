This Cookie Policy explains how Grill Seekers ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device. They help websites function and provide insights to improve user experience.

2. How we use cookies

  • Strictly necessary: to enable basic site functionality (e.g., navigation, security). These do not require consent.
  • Preference: to remember choices like your cookie consent.
  • Analytics/Marketing: not enabled by default. We will request consent before adding any analytics or marketing cookies.

3. Your choices

You can accept or decline non‑essential cookies using the banner on our site. You can also manage cookies in your browser settings.
